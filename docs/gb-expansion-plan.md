## VERHOUDINGEN DOMAIN

### Learning Progression (SLO Tussendoelen):

#### **Groep 3 - E3/M3 (30-40 items each)**
**Focus:** Visual fractions, basic parts of wholes

**E3 (Eind niveau):**
- Recognize halves (1/2) visually
- Recognize quarters (1/4) visually
- Simple fraction of quantity (half of 10 apples)
- Visual comparison (which is bigger: 1/2 or 1/4?)

**M3 (Midden niveau):**
- All E3 skills
- Recognize thirds (1/3) visually
- Fraction of shapes (color 1/4 of circle)
- Simple equivalent fractions (2/4 = 1/2 with pictures)

**Item count:** E3: 30 items, M3: 35 items

---

#### **Groep 4 - E4/M4 (40-50 items each)**
**Focus:** Fraction notation, simple operations

**E4:**
- Write fractions: 1/2, 1/4, 1/3, 1/5, 1/10
- Fraction of quantity (1/4 of 20, 1/5 of 10)
- Order fractions with same denominator (2/5 < 3/5)
- Simple equivalent: 2/4 = 1/2, 5/10 = 1/2

**M4:**
- All E4 skills
- Mixed numbers introduction (1 1/2)
- Fractions with different denominators comparison (visual aid)
- Add fractions same denominator (1/4 + 2/4)
- Introduction to decimals: 0.5 = 1/2

**Item count:** E4: 40 items, M4: 45 items

---

#### **Groep 5 - E5/M5 (50-60 items each)**
**Focus:** Decimal numbers, percentage basics

**E5:**
- Decimals to tenths (0.1, 0.5, 0.9)
- Convert: 0.5 = 1/2, 0.25 = 1/4
- Add/subtract decimals (money context: €2.50 + €1.75)
- Percentage basics: 50%, 25%, 10% (visual)
- Percentage of simple quantities (10% of 100)

**M5:**
- All E5 skills
- Decimals to hundredths (0.01, 0.25, 0.99)
- Multiply decimals by whole numbers (0.5 × 4)
- Common percentages: 1%, 5%, 20%, 75%, 100%
- Simple percentage calculations (20% of 50)
- Fraction-decimal-percentage triangle

**Item count:** E5: 50 items, M5: 55 items

---

#### **Groep 6 - E6/M6 (60-70 items each)**
**Focus:** Complex fractions, percentage applications

**E6:**
- All operations with fractions (same denominator)
- Add/subtract fractions (different denominators, simple)
- Multiply fraction by whole number (1/3 × 6)
- Decimals: all operations (+, -, ×, ÷ by whole number)
- Percentage of amounts (15% of €60)
- Percentage increase/decrease basics

**M6:**
- All E6 skills
- Multiply/divide fractions
- All operations with decimals including ÷
- Percentage: complex calculations
- Discount problems (30% korting op €45)
- Scale ratios (1:100, 1:10)

**Item count:** E6: 60 items, M6: 65 items

---

#### **Groep 7 - E7/M7 (60-70 items each)**
**Focus:** Ratio, scale, complex percentages

**E7:**
- Ratio notation (2:3)
- Scale problems (map scale 1:50000)
- Percentage: all applications
- VAT/BTW calculations (21% BTW)
- Discounts and markups
- Fraction-decimal-percentage conversions (all)

**M7:**
- All E7 skills
- Complex ratio problems
- Proportion tables
- Percentage change (from-to calculations)
- Interest calculations (simple)
- Recipe scaling (serves 4 → serves 6)

**Item count:** E7: 60 items, M7: 65 items

---

#### **Groep 8 - E8/M8 (70-80 items each)**
**Focus:** CITO preparation, complex applications

**E8:**
- All verhoudingen skills
- Complex word problems
- Percentage: reverse calculations (what was original price?)
- Scale: maps, models, drawings
- Ratio in context (mixing paint, recipes)
- Speed-distance-time basics

**M8:**
- All E8 skills
- Multi-step percentage problems
- Compound percentages (discount on discount)
- Complex scale problems
- Ratio word problems
- CITO-style applications

**Item count:** E8: 70 items, M8: 75 items

## METEN & MEETKUNDE DOMAIN

### Learning Progression (SLO Tussendoelen):

#### **Groep 3 - E3/M3 (30-40 items each)**
**Focus:** Basic measurement, shape recognition

**E3 (Meten):**
- Length: cm, m (reading ruler)
- Time: hours, half hours (analog clock)
- Money: counting coins/bills to €10
- Weight: kg (whole numbers)
- Simple comparisons (longer/shorter, heavier/lighter)

**E3 (Meetkunde):**
- Recognize shapes: circle, triangle, square, rectangle
- Count sides and corners
- Symmetry (is this symmetrical?)

**M3:**
- All E3 skills
- Length: mm introduction
- Time: quarter hours
- Money: counting to €20, making amounts
- Shapes: pentagon, hexagon
- Simple patterns

**Item count:** E3: 35 items, M3: 40 items

---

#### **Groep 4 - E4/M4 (45-55 items each)**
**Focus:** Measurement tools, shape properties

**E4 (Meten):**
- Length: mm, cm, dm, m conversions
- Time: minutes, hours, reading digital clock
- Money: calculations to €100
- Weight: g, kg basics
- Volume: liter (whole numbers)
- Calendar: days, weeks, months

**E4 (Meetkunde):**
- Shape properties (parallel lines, right angles)
- 3D shapes: cube, cuboid, sphere, cylinder
- Perimeter of simple shapes
- Position: coordinates (simple grid)

**M4:**
- All E4 skills
- Length: measurements to mm precision
- Time: time calculations (how long between 13:45 and 15:20?)
- Weight: g and kg conversions
- Volume: ml, cl, l
- Area introduction (counting squares)
- Rotation and reflection

**Item count:** E4: 45 items, M4: 50 items

---

#### **Groep 5 - E5/M5 (50-60 items each)**
**Focus:** Area, volume, complex shapes

**E5 (Meten):**
- All metric conversions within system
- Time: hours, minutes, seconds calculations
- Money: complex calculations, change
- Area: rectangle (l × w)
- Perimeter calculations

**E5 (Meetkunde):**
- Triangles: types (equilateral, isosceles, right)
- Angles: right angle, acute, obtuse
- Symmetry lines (multiple)
- 3D shapes: cone, pyramid
- Nets of 3D shapes

**M5:**
- All E5 skills
- Area: complex shapes (L-shapes)
- Volume: cubic units (counting blocks)
- Angles: measuring with protractor basics
- Coordinates: negative numbers introduction
- Scale drawings basics

**Item count:** E5: 50 items, M5: 55 items

---

#### **Groep 6 - E6/M6 (55-65 items each)**
**Focus:** Formulas, calculations, 3D geometry

**E6 (Meten):**
- All metric system (km to mm, kg to mg)
- Time: complex calculations across days
- Area: triangle, parallelogram
- Perimeter: circles (circumference)
- Volume: cuboid (l × w × h)

**E6 (Meetkunde):**
- Angles: sum in triangles, quadrilaterals
- Protractor: measuring and drawing
- Circle: radius, diameter, center
- Transformations: translation, rotation, reflection
- Coordinates: all four quadrants

**M6:**
- All E6 skills
- Area: circle (πr²), complex shapes
- Volume: irregular shapes
- Pythagoras (introduction)
- Scale: enlargement/reduction
- 3D coordinates

**Item count:** E6: 55 items, M6: 60 items

---

#### **Groep 7 - E7/M7 (60-70 items each)**

**E7 (Meten):**
- Metric system: all conversions fluently

**E7 (Meetkunde):**

**M7:**

#### **Groep 8 - E8/M8 (70-80 items each)**

- **Meten & Meetkunde:** `gb_groep[N]_meetkunde_[level].json`
